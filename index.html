About the Event

Algothon 2018 @ BlackRock is a 24-hour hackathon focused on implementing alpha generating algorithms. During the event, participants will have the opportunity to attend workshops conducted by BlackRock staff and Imperial College Algorithmic Trading Society committee members. Distinguished guest speakers from various institutions have also been invited to give talks on areas in the field of machine learning. Mentors from BlackRock will be present throughout the event to help teams with their hacks.

The hackathon will run throughout the weekend of 20 - 21 October 2018 at the BlackRock Office in London. This event is sponsored by BlackRock and organised by the Imperial College Algorithmic Trading Society.

FAQs

Q. Do we have to form teams before the event?

No, there will be time for team formation before the hacking starts.


Q. Is it free to attend?

Yes, there is no entry fee to the event. However, participants are expected to cover their own travel and accomodation expenses.

No, travel will not be reimbursed.
